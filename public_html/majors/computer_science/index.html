Computer Science


General Information

Computer Science is a broad field that includes the study of computers, programming languages, algorithms, data structures, and computer systems. It also involves the theory, design, development, and application of software and hardware components that are used to solve problems and tasks. It can also include the study of artificial intelligence, databases, computer networks, graphics, and human-computer interaction.
