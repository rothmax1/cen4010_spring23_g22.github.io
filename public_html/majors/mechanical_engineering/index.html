Mechanical Engineering


What Is It?

Mechanical engineering focuses on the design, analysis, and development of mechanical systems, machines, and devices. It involves the application of physics, mathematics, and material science principles to the design and manufacture of machines, structures, and components.

They design and develop various air-moving products such as wind turbines, automobiles, and aircraft. They use mathematical models and computer simulations to analyze how air flows around different shapes and structures, enabling them to enhance the products' performance and efficiency.
