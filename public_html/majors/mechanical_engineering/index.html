Mechanical Engineering


General Information

Mechanical engineering focuses on the design, analysis, and development of mechanical systems, machines, and devices. It involves the application of physics, mathematics, and material science principles to the design and manufacture of machines, structures, and components.

  4. Biomedical Engineer
    5. Biomedical engineers develop and build medical equipment and products that can improve healthcare. They are responsible for ensuring the proper installation and maintenance of the equipment to ensure it works correctly, and they also provide training to medical professionals on how to use the devices. Biomedical engineers may also develop software that can help diagnose or treat medical issues.
    Average Salary in US: $97,410

    • Annott Laboratories

  5. Material Engineer
    6. A materials engineer analyzes different materials, including metals and plastics, to determine their suitability for use in various products such as aircraft wings or biomedical devices. They conduct research to develop new materials that meet specific requirements, such as mechanical, electrical, or chemical properties.
    Average Salary in US: $70,881-$86,258
