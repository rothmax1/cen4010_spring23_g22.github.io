Electrical Engineering


General Information

Electrical engineering focuses on the study and practical application of electricity, electronics, and electromagnetism. It involves designing, testing, and overseeing the production of electrical equipment, including communication systems, electric motors, power generation equipment, and navigation systems. They are also involved in the design and implementation of systems for generating, transmitting, and distributing electrical power.
