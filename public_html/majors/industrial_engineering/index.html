Industrial Engineering


What Is It?

Industrial engineering focuses on the design, development, and optimization of complex systems, processes, and organizations. Industrial engineers work to improve efficiency, productivity, and quality while reducing costs and waste in various industries.



What Can I Do With It?

Process Engineer
A process engineer applies engineering principles to design and enhance processes that are utilized in the production of goods and services. They utilize their expertise in engineering, science, and mathematics to detect and eliminate inefficiencies and enhance the overall effectiveness of a process. Process engineers work in various industries, such as manufacturing, chemical, and pharmaceuticals, and may be involved in developing new processes or improving existing ones. They are also responsible for taking into account the environmental impact of their processes and complying with relevant regulations. Ergonomists work in various industries, such as healthcare, manufacturing, and transportation, and may be responsible for designing workplaces, choosing equipment, and developing training programs.
